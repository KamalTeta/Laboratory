About me

I am an Aarhus based artist, who is starting to come into my own, after having honed my craft for many years.
This comes as I am now pushing myself to show more of my work, and to live a more creative life.
I am interested in experimenting with multiple styles and mediums, but my specialty is in drawing and painting.

I work a full-time job, but I am always open to work on side projects and opportunities.
